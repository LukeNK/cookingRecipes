Preparation

Finely chop the vegtables. Remember to separate the tomato from other vegtables. You can use other vegtables if you want.

Cooking

  1. Get a relatively deep or big pot that can handle the amount of vegtables.
  2. Fry the beef using high heat until the water evaporates.
  3. Put the carrots, onions, and garlics into the pot and continue to fry.
  4. Fry until there is sucs at the bottom of the pot, deglaze the sucs layer using milk. Pour the milk and shave the bottom lightly until clean.
  5. Pour the tomatoes into the pot, season with tomato sauce and ketchup. You may add broth mix powder to create additional flavour.
  6. Let the sauce simmer on low heat for 3 hours or more. Stir the sauce every 30 minutes to prevent the sauce from over-cooking.
  7. In the last 30 minutes, add spices, pepper, and seasoning.
  8. (optional) Before serving, glaze a little bit of olive oil over the sauce. Add basil for garnishing.
